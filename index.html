Usability test of the PhD application for Human Factors at Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University

Worked in a team of five to run a total of six participants through various tasks necessary to complete the PhD application process.

  • Three users were tested remotely using User Testing
  • Three were tested face-to-face through Morae.
  • Users were asked to complete the System Usability Scale to assess the usability of the process.

Our results showed an average of 48.3 out of 100 on the system usability scale.

  • The findings were presented to members on the ERAU admissions team.

    • For more information check out our presentation.

Usability Analysis of the Spotify desktop application

Conducted a usability analysis of the Spotify desktop application to assess the ease of use of the program.

  • Total of 5 participants
  • Prompted them to complete a variety of tasks utilizing different functions of the application
  • Participants completed the System Usability Scale

Our results showed an average of 77.3 out of 100 on the system usability scale.

  • Results varied based on exposure to the app

Phlebotomist Robot Design

Created a concept design and a mockup of a robot to be used in trauma environments to assist with drawing blood, distributing IVs, and storing medical information.

  • Team members consulted with subject matter experts along with distributing a usability survey to collect feedback on the original concept design.
  • The team redesigned the robot according to the results of the survey and SME feedback to maximize efficiency and performance of the robot.

Human-Computer Interaction

Whether auditory, visual, or physical, human-computer interaction is concerned with both the flow of information from the user into a machine and output from the machine to a user. Legibility, predictive aiding, and the minimization of of interaction cost are all factors that create an effective interface.
